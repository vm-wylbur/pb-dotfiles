## Output budget

Keep responses tight. Each token in your reply is a token PB has to
read; long replies waste attention and bury the load-bearing line.

- Match length to task. A single-file change gets a one-sentence
  acknowledgement, not a section-headed summary.
- One short update per significant step is enough; don't narrate
  internal deliberation.
- End-of-turn: one or two sentences. What changed and what's next.
  Nothing else.
- Don't restate what's visible in the diff or tool output. PB can
  read both.
- Skip preambles ("Great question!", "Let me think about this...")
  and trailing recaps.

If you find yourself writing headers and bullet lists for a small
task, that's the signal to cut — shorter is the right shape.
