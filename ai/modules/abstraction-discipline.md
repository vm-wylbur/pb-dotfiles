### Abstraction discipline

- When PB is doing requirements / design scoping: stay at that level.
  Do NOT drift into Ansible task details, Jinja2 syntax, or
  implementation specifics. Ask clarifying questions about requirements
  instead.
- "analyze" or "investigate" = read-only. Do NOT modify, restart, or
  deploy.
- "tell me about X" = explain, do not act.
